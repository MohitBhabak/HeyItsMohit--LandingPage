Medicean Siderea

As part of the Gerald O'Neill Space Settlement Contest, teams are tasked with creating a comprehensive scientific report outlining the function & construction of a Space Settlement, designed to house humanity away from Earth. As the Vice-Lead of my 9-member group, I led the design of the settlement, researched & described the physics behind it, outlined the social & political systems of the society, & devised innovative technologies to ensure the best quality of life for the residents.

Over the course of 8 months, I led the team through a thorough design & scientific process that produced what was undoubtedly the most in-depth & thorough scientific piece of work of our lives thus far. Employing agile project management techniques, I led the team through successful sprints & successfully overcame the several challenges.

With 5200 submissions from 27 countries & 27000+ overall participants, it was surely a challenge. My team finished Top 5 in our Grade 12 category, helping put our school on the map. We were even invited to attend the International Space Development Conference ran by the NSS in Los Angeles. The whole process & reward was an amazing experience.
