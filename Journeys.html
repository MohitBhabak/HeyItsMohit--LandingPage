Journeys - JamHacks 2024

There are 2.5 billion obese individuals worldwide who often lack the motivation to walk or jog outside and explore their communities. This lack of physical activity adversely affects mental health, lifestyle, and can lead to medical issues and social exclusion. Journeys is designed to tackle these issues by providing consistent motivation for people to venture outside their homes, enjoy various challenges and quests, and earn rewards. Our mobile app not only tracks essential statistics such as distance traveled and calories burned but also enhances the experience by incorporating location-based landmarks and checkpoints. As users explore different areas, they can earn unique NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) as rewards for visiting each landmark. These NFTs can be collected & traded as well using OpenSea!

I led the design & product-side of things of the application. Utilizing Figma, I protoyped the UI interfaces, created a user-flow map, designed the logo & other key product materials, & enhanced the UX wherever possible.

Check out the Figma prototype here & feel free to browse the Github Repo as well!
